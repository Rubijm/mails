Se buscan agentes inmobiliarios en Buenos Aires para Show de TV de USA

¿Eres un agente inmobiliario conocido en el mundo inmobiliario de Buenos Aires y te gustaría aparecer en la serie de televisión de los Estados Unidos “Bang for your bucks”? Hace poco la productora asociada del show HGTV Jeanna Friederich me contactó pidiendo si podía ayudarla a encontrar algunos agentes inmobiliarios para el show. Si te interesa participar debes estar en Buenos Aires durante dos días para los horarios de grabación de agosto 25 y 26, y hablar inglés. Si eres elegido para el programa no sólo te verán a ti y a tu negocio internacionalmente, sino que también se ofrece una compensación.

Aquí hay algunos detalles del programa y la manera de aplicar. ¡La mejor de las suertes y esperamos verte pronto en la tele!

One of America’s hit shows on the Home & Garden Television (HGTV) channel, “Bang For Your Buck,” is looking for a fun, enthusiastic, and knowledgeable real estate agent in the Buenos Aires area to feature as our local Real Estate Expert for one episode of the show! Your job will be to tour 3 recent home renovations alongside our Design Expert, and critique each of the homes to see which homeowner will see the greatest return on their investment. The show will provide viewers with expert advice, answers on remodeling values, and inspiring ideas for design.
HGTV is America’s leader in home and lifestyle programming. It is distributed to more than 89 million U.S. households and is one of cable’s top-rated networks. HGTV.com is the nation’s leading online home and garden destination that attracts an average of 5.2 million unique visitors per month. HGTV owns 33 percent of HGTV Canada and provides much of the Canadian network’s daily programming. The network’s branded programming also can be seen in 47 other countries and its selected programming is available to service men and women on board Navy ships and through American Forces Radio and Television Service (AFRTS), which services more than 1,000 outlets in over 175 countries.
Filming for this one episode will take place on August 25th & 26th. To get an idea of what you’ll be doing if selected, visit http://www.hgtv.com/hgtv-bang-for-your-buck/videos/index.html to watch episodes of the show.

*Compensation will be provided*
If you’re interested, please send a photo of yourself and your resume/experience (including number of years as a Realtor) ASAP to:

Jenna Friederich, Associate Producer
jfriederich@highnoontv.com
Once I receive your photo and information I will tell you what the next step is.
Thank you!
